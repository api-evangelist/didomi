Didomi compares three website compliance scanning tools for different needs: XL Scan (audits up to 20,000 pages in one pass for CMP migrations and large sites), Advanced Compliance Monitoring (up to 150 pages per scan, scheduled as often as hourly to detect regressions), and the free monthly Compliance Report that scans one page at a time as a quick diagnostic. The post positions the options along a spectrum from one-time large-scale audits to ongoing operational monitoring.
