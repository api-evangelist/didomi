This interview profiles Cyrielle Jougla, who spent five years at Didomi progressing from Customer Onboarding Manager to Team Lead and finally to Technical Support Engineer. She discusses her career evolution, transition into technical support, and how Didomi's remote-first culture enabled her to work across five countries without sacrificing career growth. Cyrielle plans to deepen her technical expertise while building bridges between Didomi and Addingwell support teams.
