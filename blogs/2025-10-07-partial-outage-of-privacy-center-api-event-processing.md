Oct 7 , 08:30 UTC Resolved - On October 7th, we experienced a partial outage in our analytics and consent storage pipeline between approximately 8:50 and 9:51 UTC. This issue resulted in a failure to store a portion of events, including a very limited number consent.given events, sent from our SDKs during this window. This incident did not affect the behavior of the consent notice on client websites or applications.
