Aug 15 , 09:00 UTC Resolved - On August 17, 2023, we received reports from some clients that their Web SDK Google AMP notices were generating Google-related errors (e.g., 1.2, 3.3 and 2.1.A errors; also 1.1, 2.1.B and 2.2.B errors) . The cause of the errors was traced to the consent string only containing legitimate interest but without consent information being stored. As a result, the number of Limited Ads being displayed increased.
