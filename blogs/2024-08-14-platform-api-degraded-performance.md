Aug 14 , 23:33 UTC Resolved - The incident has been resolved. During the incident, the Platform API (responsible for managing notice configurations for the Didomi Console and API clients) and the Didomi Console were temporarily impacted with higher latency or partial unavailability. All other services, including the Consents API, consent notices, and consent collection, were not affected and were operating normally.
